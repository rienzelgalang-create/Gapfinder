The Science: Bayesian Knowledge Tracing

GapFinder doesn't just grade tests; it predicts learning. We use a sophisticated AI algorithm known as Bayesian Knowledge Tracing (BKT) to estimate a student's true mastery of a subject.

1. Observe Behavior

When a student answers a question, the system observes the result. However, getting a question right doesn't guarantee mastery (they might have guessed), and getting it wrong doesn't guarantee ignorance (they might have slipped up).

2. Update Probabilities

Using a Hidden Markov Model, our BKT engine takes that observation and updates the probability of the student's Latent Skill. It mathematically separates actual understanding from guessing and careless mistakes.

3. Predict Mastery

As the student takes more quizzes, the algorithm becomes more confident. It provides the Teacher Dashboard with a highly accurate "Mastery Percentage," showing exactly when a student has truly learned a concept.
